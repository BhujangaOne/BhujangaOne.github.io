Welcome to My Dev Journey

You found my blog / portfolio on my journey to becoming a Blockchain Developer

During the CoVid-19 pandemic I rediscovered my passion for software development. I commited myself to a daily learning routine, so I can reach my goal to work as a software developer as soon as possible. Since February 2021 I am studying Blockchain Development at the Ivan on Tech Academy. The more I learn about Blockchain technology, the more I can see the tremendous impact and potential benefits it holds for future - and present - generations.
